BCH Mining Information

BCH mining is the process of verifying transactions on the Bitcoin Cash network and adding them to the blockchain. Miners are rewarded with newly minted BCH for their work.

To mine BCH, you need to connect to a mining pool using your mining hardware. The mining pool will provide you with work to do, and you will submit your completed work to the pool for verification.

When mining BCH, it's important to consider the mining difficulty, as well as the current block reward and block time. These factors can affect the profitability of your mining operation.
