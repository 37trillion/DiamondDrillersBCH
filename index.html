What is Diamond Drillers BCH?

Diamond Drillers BCH is an experimental project to create a mining pool for Bitcoin Cash. Our goal is to provide a reliable and profitable mining experience for our users while also contributing to the security and decentralization of the Bitcoin Cash network.

To get started with mining, follow the instructions on our Mining Instructions page. For more information about Bitcoin Cash and other resources, visit our Summary and Links page.
