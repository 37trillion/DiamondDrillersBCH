About Bitcoin Cash

Bitcoin Cash (BCH) is a cryptocurrency that was created in 2017 as a fork of Bitcoin. It was created to address the scalability issues of Bitcoin and to increase the block size limit, which allows for faster and cheaper transactions. Bitcoin Cash has a growing community of supporters who believe that it has the potential to become a widely used digital currency.

For more information about Bitcoin Cash, please visit the BCH subreddit or the Bitcoin Cash website.
