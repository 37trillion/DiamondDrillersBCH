Start Mining Bitcoin Cash

Follow these steps to connect to our stratum:

  1. Download and install a Bitcoin Cash mining software such as CGMiner, BFGMiner or Easyminer.
  2. Open the software and configure your mining device to connect to the stratum: stratum+tcp://192.168.0.58:8333
  3. Enter your Diamond Drillers BCH username and password.
  4. Start mining!

If you need help with mining, please contact our customer support team.
