Summary

Diamond Drillers BCH is a Bitcoin Cash mining pool that uses the stratum protocol for efficient and fast mining. To connect to our stratum, follow these steps:

  1. Download and install a Bitcoin Cash mining software such as CGMiner, BFGMiner or Easyminer.
  2. Open the software and configure your mining device to connect to the stratum: stratum+tcp://192.168.0.58:8333
  3. Enter your Diamond Drillers BCH Wallet address
  4. Start mining!

With our pool, you can start mining Bitcoin Cash and earn rewards based on your mining contribution. The more you mine, the more rewards you will earn. To get started with mining, you can follow these basic steps:

  1. Choose a Bitcoin Cash mining software and configure it to connect to our stratum.
  2. Connect to the network
  3. Start mining and monitor your progress.
  4. Collect your rewards and withdraw your earnings.
